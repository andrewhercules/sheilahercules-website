About Sheila Hercules

I’m human!

I've been around the sun a few times or so! Married with four grown boys! Grandmother of one little boy! Life has had its ups and downs for sure – I have survived and thrived!

I began my career in the Direct Sales Industry as a bit of fluke! I was already working as an Executive Assistant to a CEO and was paid pretty well but something kept urging me that I could do more, that I could impact more people if only I would step outside my comfort zone.

I had done Direct Sales as an independent “Sales Agent” for a few companies – some start-ups and others pretty established and had some success but on a whim I sent in a resume to AVON – I mean that was a big and successful company and just maybe they would hire me – and they did! And I LOVED IT!

From that early beginning I went on to work for several Party Plan companies and Multi Level Marketing companies. Some were wonderful experiences! And some were not!

BUT, I bet I have talked to thousands of independent “Sales Agents” and Leaders, and have walked alongside their journeys as a coach, trainer and/or mentor.

That brings me to today! I have been there, done that and now I want to help you!
